CCBD2014 Registration Notes:

 A. REGISTRATION TYPE

 Please note that ONE Full registration must be paid for EACH paper to be published in the proceedings.

Full registration includes:

  • Publication of one accepted paper in the proceedings
  • A copy of the proceedings
  • Access to all conference sessions
  • Lunch, coffee breaks, banquet and welcome reception
  • Program brochure and conference goodies (e.g., bags/paper)

Please note that if your payment is not received by that date, the paper will be removed from the proceedings. This deadline will be strictly enforced.
