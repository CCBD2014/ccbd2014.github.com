INTRODUCTION

Cloud Computing provides a scalable services consumption and cost-effective delivery platform for services computing. Big data technologies offer the promise of providing valuable insights for science, engineering, medicine, finance and business. Both technologies continue to converge and mutual promotion. The International Conference on Cloud Computing and Big Data (CCBD2014) is a cross-area international forum on cloud computing and big data. CCBD will be held on November 12-14, 2014 at Wuhan University, Wuhan, China.

Call for Paper can be downloaded at here.
